Our Goals

What we've done and will do for Stuyvesant

Our platform centers on the concept of Work Hard | Play Hard. First term senior year, your primary concern will be college, so we our focus will be on doing as much as we can to ease the pressure and difficulties of the fall semester. Second term, it's time to relax and play hard, and we will ensure that you have an enjoyable last few months at Stuyvesant.
